RABBIT

The rabbit is a small, furry mammal known for its long ears, strong hind legs, and short, fluffy tail. It is a herbivore and loves to eat grass, vegetables, and leafy plants. Rabbits are very fast and use their speed to escape from predators. They live in burrows or warrens, often in groups, and are most active during dawn and dusk. Rabbits are gentle and shy animals, often kept as pets. They communicate using body language, soft sounds, and thumping their hind legs. With their cute appearance, rabbits are loved by people all over the world.
