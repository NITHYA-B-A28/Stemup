COW

The cow is a gentle, domesticated animal raised around the world for milk, meat, and labor. It is a herbivore, mainly feeding on grass, hay, and grains. Cows have a unique digestive system with four stomach compartments that help them break down tough plant materials. They are social animals and often form close bonds with other cows. In many cultures, cows are considered sacred or highly valued. They communicate through mooing and body language. Cows play an important role in agriculture and provide essential dairy products like milk, cheese, and butter.
