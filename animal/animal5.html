TIGER

The tiger is the largest member of the cat family and is known for its power and striking orange coat with black stripes. It is a solitary and territorial animal, often found in forests, grasslands, and mangroves across Asia. Tigers are carnivores and skilled hunters, primarily feeding on deer, wild boar, and other large prey. There are several subspecies of tigers, including the Bengal, Siberian, and Sumatran tigers. Sadly, they are endangered due to habitat loss, poaching, and human-wildlife conflict. Conservation efforts are ongoing to protect their shrinking populations.
