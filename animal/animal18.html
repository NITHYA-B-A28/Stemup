BUTTERFLY

The butterfly is a beautiful insect known for its colorful wings and graceful flight. It begins life as a caterpillar and transforms into a butterfly through a process called metamorphosis. Butterflies feed on nectar from flowers using their long, straw-like tongues. They play an important role in pollination, helping plants reproduce. Butterflies are active during the day and are often seen fluttering in gardens, meadows, and forests. Their delicate wings are covered in tiny scales that form unique patterns. Butterflies symbolize change, hope, and beauty in many cultures around the world.
