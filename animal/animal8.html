FOX

The fox is a small to medium-sized, intelligent mammal known for its sharp features and bushy tail. It belongs to the dog family and is found in forests, grasslands, mountains, and even urban areas. Foxes are omnivores, feeding on small animals, insects, fruits, and sometimes garbage. The most common species is the red fox, known for its reddish coat and white underbelly. Foxes are mostly nocturnal and are excellent hunters with keen hearing. They are also known in folklore for their clever and cunning nature.
