Horse

The horse is a strong and graceful animal that has been a loyal companion to humans for thousands of years. Known for its speed and endurance, it has been used for transportation, farming, and sport. Horses come in many breeds, colors, and sizes, each suited to different tasks. They are herbivores and mainly eat grass, hay, and grains. Horses are social animals that form close bonds with humans and other horses. They communicate through body language, sounds, and facial expressions. Their beauty and strength make them one of the most admired animals in the world.
