DEER

The deer is a graceful, herbivorous animal found in forests, grasslands, and mountains across many parts of the world. It is known for its slender legs, keen senses, and, in many species, antlers found on males. Deer primarily feed on grass, leaves, and fruits. They are social animals and often live in herds for protection. Their speed and agility help them escape predators like tigers and wolves. Deer play an important role in the ecosystem by helping with seed dispersal and maintaining plant balance.
