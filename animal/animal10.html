LEOPARD

The leopard is a powerful and agile big cat known for its spotted coat and stealthy hunting skills. It lives in a wide range of habitats, including forests, mountains, and savannas across Africa and parts of Asia. Leopards are solitary animals and mainly hunt at night, feeding on deer, monkeys, and smaller animals. They are excellent climbers and often drag their prey up trees to avoid scavengers. Despite their strength, leopards are elusive and prefer to avoid human contact. Their beautiful rosette-patterned fur helps them blend into their surroundings. Sadly, habitat loss and poaching threaten their survival.
