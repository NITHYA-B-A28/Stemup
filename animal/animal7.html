SQUIRREL

A squirrel is a small, agile rodent commonly found in forests, parks, and urban areas. It has a bushy tail, sharp claws, and strong hind legs for jumping and climbing trees. Squirrels are known for their habit of gathering and storing nuts for winter. They are very alert and quick, helping them escape predators easily. Squirrels mainly feed on nuts, seeds, fruits, and sometimes insects. Their playful and curious nature makes them a favorite among wildlife watchers. There are many species of squirrels, including tree, ground, and flying squirrels.
