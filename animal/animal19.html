CROW

The crow is a highly intelligent black bird known for its sharp mind and problem-solving abilities. It belongs to the corvid family and is found in many parts of the world. Crows are omnivores, eating everything from grains and fruits to small animals and garbage. They are social birds and often seen in groups called murders. Crows have a loud, cawing voice and can mimic sounds, including human speech. They are known to use tools and even recognize human faces. Crows play an important role in the ecosystem by cleaning up waste and controlling pests.
