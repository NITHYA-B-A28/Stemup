Burger

Burgers are tasty, satisfying sandwiches perfect for quick meals, combining soft buns, flavorful fillings, and endless creativity.

Veg Burger

is a classic option with a crispy vegetable patty, fresh lettuce, tomato, onion, and tangy sauces.

Paneer Burger

features a thick, spiced paneer patty, paired with veggies and creamy mayo for a rich, Indian-style twist.

Fruit Burger

offers a refreshing take, made with fresh fruits, sweet cream, and soft buns, perfect for those who enjoy a light, unique flavor.

Cheese Burger

is packed with a juicy patty and loads of melted cheese, delivering a rich, savory taste that melts in your mouth.
