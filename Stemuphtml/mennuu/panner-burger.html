Panner Burger

Paneer Burger is a flavorful twist on the classic, featuring a thick, spiced paneer (cottage cheese) patty that's crispy on the outside and soft inside. Served in fresh buns with lettuce, onions, and creamy sauces, it delivers a rich and satisfying bite. The combination of Indian spices and soft paneer makes it a favorite for vegetarians craving something hearty. It’s both filling and full of flavor, perfect for lunch or a quick snack. Price: ₹89 – a tasty fusion of tradition and fast food.
