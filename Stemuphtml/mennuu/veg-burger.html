Veg Burger

Veg Burger is a classic delight with a crispy vegetable patty tucked between soft, toasted buns. Layered with fresh lettuce, onions, tomatoes, and creamy sauces, it offers a perfect mix of crunch and flavor. The patty is made from a blend of mashed veggies and spices, giving it a satisfying, homemade taste. It’s a wholesome and filling choice for quick meals or light bites. Price: ₹69 – delicious, affordable, and always satisfying.
